Who We Are

Turning Plots Into Landmark Homes

At Malwa Builders, every home is a promise kept. Based in Jagraon, we are a team of architects, engineers and interior designers obsessed with detail — from structural strength to the smallest moulding on your elevation.

We believe great construction is invisible until you live in it. That's why we plan thoroughly, build honestly, and finish beautifully — delivering luxury houses, bungalows and farmhouses that stand out and stand strong.

  • Proper inside-out elevation detailing
  • Thorough architecture & structural planning
  • Premium materials & expert craftsmanship
  • Transparent timelines and budgets
